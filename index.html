About Me

I'm a web developer and designer based in Pennsylvania, with a passion for building digital experiences that help real businesses connect with their communities.

With a Bachelor's in Web & Interactive Media from Pennsylvania College of Technology and a Master's in Software Engineering from Penn State, I bring both creative design sensibilities and solid engineering fundamentals to every project.

From comic shops to reptile zoos—I've helped some of Pennsylvania's best-known businesses grow online. See the work.

When I'm not coding, you might find me fishing, hiking, or playing video games.
